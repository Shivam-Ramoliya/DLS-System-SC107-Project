Who Invented DLS Method?

Frank Duckworth and Tony Lewis

Steven_Stern

  • Frank Duckworth and Tony Lewis introduced a DLS method
  • It was first introduced in 1997 and later in 1999 it was accepted and adopted by ICC.
  • After the retirement of Duckworth and Lewis, Steven Stern became the Custodian of The Method and the method was given the current name i.e. DLS(Duckworth-Lewis-Stern) Method.
